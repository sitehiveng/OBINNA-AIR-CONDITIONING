Company Overview

A trusted air conditioning partner in Surulere, Lagos

OBINNA AIR CONDITIONING is a trusted air conditioning company based in Surulere, Lagos, specialising in the sales, installation, repair, maintenance and servicing of residential and commercial air conditioning systems. We are committed to delivering reliable, efficient and affordable cooling solutions using skilled technicians, quality equipment and excellent customer service.

Whether it's a new installation, routine maintenance, or emergency repair, we ensure every project is completed with professionalism and attention to detail.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable and high-quality air conditioning solutions that enhance comfort for homes, offices and businesses while delivering exceptional customer service and lasting value.

Our Vision

To become one of Lagos's most trusted and preferred air conditioning service providers, recognised for professionalism, quality workmanship, innovation and customer satisfaction.
